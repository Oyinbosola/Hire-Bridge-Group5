Branch workflow

main → production
(this is never to be touched except by me)
develop → integration branch 
(where the team mates will branch from and pull to)
feature branches → individual work
 (your individual branches)

Example:
feature/login-page
feature/recruiter-login-page
feature/candidate-dashboard
feature/signout

Our page starts on the welcome page,and then users get to login as Recruiter lr Candidate 
If the recruiter page is chosen they can immediately sign up as a recruiter and register their respective companies 
After that they will be led to their dashboard 
If user is a candidate i.e a job seeker then they would sign up as a candidate and will be guided to the candidate signup page 
After that they will be redirected to the candidate profile info pages
They will put their basic information, Education level,job experiences,resume and job preferences 
After that they will be guided to their dashboard as well,where they can go to the job listings and apply for a job,or their job status and see the progress of their applications so far
